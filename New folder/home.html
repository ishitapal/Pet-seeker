Process to adopt a pet

Have a friend who leaves a paw print on your heart.

Pet Selection

Selecting the right pet for yourself is the most important decision one has to make before adopting one as this is almost a decade-long commitment. The primary reason that pet owners might one day relinquish their pets is because of the unrealistic expectations that they had when they first entered into a pet ownership. Therefore, before getting started, be certain that you understand the time, commitment, and expense that you will be undertaking over the next 15 to 20 years.Read More

Meeting Authority

Adopting a pet is very similar to adopting a child. So a background check would also be done to make sure that you have no previous criminal record and have financial support as well. To check wether you are fit to adopt a pet Home visit will also be done.This is done to ensure that the animals are going to get along and to set up the potential adopters for success with bringing a new animal into their home. There is never a 100% success rate, but these meetings on neutral ground are an effective way of gauging the animal’s reactions.As . Read More

Application Form Filling

Adoption Application Form is designed for adopters to provide their personal and contact details, history of caring pets at home, information related to their family and home environment with their consent to terms and conditions.This is to verify they have the right environment and lifestyle and are equipped to take care of a dog. It is a digital form that has to be filled out on a website or can be sent via email. Read More

Bringing a new family memeber.

After passing the application stage, the potential adopters then go to the shelter to meet one or several pets they are interested in. Very often, people come to a shelter unprepared and just adopt whichever pet they happen to fall in love with, without any consideration for how compatible the pet is with their expected lifestyle, living costs, activity level, age, etc.,
